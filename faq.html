Our Curriculum and Programs

At Noisy Monkeys Academy program environemnt, these six distinct areas constitute the prepared environment:

  • What programs do you offer and what is your staff ratio?

    We offer Infant and Toddler (0-36 months), Full Day Early Learning 3-5, 3-5 preschool and School Age program.

    Toddler (0-36 months) ratio is 1 to 4. Preschoolers (3 – 5) ratio is 1 to 8 and for School age (5-12 years) ratio is 1 to 10.

  • What is the centre’s illness policy?
    Children that fall ill with anything contagious (fever, vomiting, diarrhea, eye discharge, rashes, etc.) must stay home until they have been symptom free for 24 hours. We want to ensure that each child is well enough to enjoy and participate in all aspects of the programs.

  • Does my child need to be toilet trained (3-5 program)?
    Our 3-5 Program is not mandatory to be potty trained; we do hope parents and teachers work together to help your child potty train. *For children who are not potty trained, must have pull ups.

  • Will I receive report about what my child did during the day?
    Parents will receive a report at the end of each day. The report reflects a child’s food and liquid intake, diaper changes, sleep time(s) and a general overview of how the child’s day went.
